I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Korea University advised by Wonjun Lee. Prior to joining NetLab in March 2012, I completed my undergrad at Korea University.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in networked systems with a current focus on improving the network performance of data centers and computing clusters to accelerate modern cloud applications like web search, key-value store, data analytics, and AI services. My recent projects focus on designing efficient buffer management solutions for data center switches and developing novel solutions using emerging networking hardware like P4-enabled programmable switches and Smart NICs.
